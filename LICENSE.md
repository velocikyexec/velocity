## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

- ❌ **Attach Button Does Nothing**
  The Roblox client may not be fully loaded yet. Wait until you are inside the experience lobby, then try the attach action in Velocity Executor again. Running as administrator also resolves permission conflicts on Windows.

- ❌ **Scripts Fail to Execute After Update**
  A cached script may reference deprecated API calls. Clear the script cache at `C:/Velocity/cache/` and re-download from the Velocity Executor Github script hub. This keeps everything compatible with the newest build.

- ❌ **Velocity Executor Apk Crashes on Launch**
  Ensure your Android version meets the minimum requirement of 8.0 and that at least 3 GB of RAM is free. Reinstalling the Velocity Executor Apk from the official source often resolves corrupted installs.

- ❌ **Auto Execute Scripts Not Firing**
  Confirm the `autoExecute` flag is set to `true` in `config.json` and that your scripts are placed inside the correct `autoexec/` directory. Velocity Executor Auto Execute only triggers files with a `.lua` or `.luau` extension.

- ❌ **iOS Build Stops Working After Seven Days**
  Free Apple ID certificates expire weekly. Re-sign the `.ipa` with your sideloading tool and re-install. This is an Apple limitation, not a Velocity Executor issue, but it is the most common support question.

- ❌ **High Memory Usage During Long Sessions**
  The performance overlay may accumulate logging data. Toggle it off, then re-enable it to reset the buffer. Also consider increasing `scriptCacheLimit` if Velocity Executor keeps reloading large scripts from disk.
